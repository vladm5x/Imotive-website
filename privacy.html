Last updated: April 28, 2026

What we collect

When you sign up, we may store your email, basic profile answers, saved scholarships, and application progress so the product can show relevant matches.

We use Supabase for authentication and data storage. Scholarship interaction data (saves, status updates) is associated with your account and stored securely.

How we use it

We use profile data to rank scholarships, improve filters, debug product issues, and understand what funding students are trying to find.

We do not sell your data, share it with third parties for advertising, or use it for any purpose beyond improving the scholarship matching experience.

Your choices

You can contact us at hello@imotive.se to request access, correction, or deletion of your prototype account data.

You can also clear your local profile data at any time through your browser settings — local storage data is separate from your Supabase account data.
