Frequently asked

Is iMotive free for students?

Yes. iMotive is completely free for students. We don't charge for access, hide results behind a paywall, or promote sponsored scholarships over relevant ones.

How do you verify scholarships?

We collect scholarship data from official university pages, foundation websites, and verified sources — then normalize and cross-reference details before they appear in results.

Can I suggest a scholarship to add?

Absolutely. Email us the URL and basic details at hello@imotive.se. If it meets our verification criteria, we'll add it to the database.

How does the matching algorithm work?

We score each scholarship against your profile — field of study, degree level, nationality, funding need, and interests — then rank them so the most relevant opportunities appear first.
