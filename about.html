Our principles
01 — Accuracy

Verified data only

Every scholarship in our database is checked against the official source. We cross-reference deadlines, amounts, and eligibility requirements before showing them to students.

02 — Relevance

Matched to your profile

A scholarship list 500 items long is useless. We rank opportunities by how well they match your field, level, nationality, and funding situation — so you see what matters first.

03 — Transparency

No hidden agenda

We don't charge for access, promote paid listings, or hide opportunities. iMotive's goal is to surface all relevant funding — not just the ones that pay to be found.
