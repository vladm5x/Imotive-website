Scholarship discovery should feel less like detective work.

iMotive is built for students who know funding exists somewhere, but do not have hours to dig through scattered university pages, PDFs, and foundation websites.

what we are building

A cleaner route from search to application

We collect scholarship data, normalize the important details, and help students understand which opportunities fit their profile before they spend time applying.

Built around real student friction

  • Search by programme, level, field, nationality, funding need, amount, deadline, and effort.
  • Save opportunities and track what information each application still needs.
  • Give universities better visibility into what students are trying to find.
